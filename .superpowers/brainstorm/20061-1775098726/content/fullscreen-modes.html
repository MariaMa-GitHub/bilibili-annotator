Sidebar in Different Video Modes

How should the sidebar behave across Bilibili's four viewing modes?

A

Works in normal + widescreen only

In full webpage and fullscreen modes, sidebar hides automatically. A small floating "📋" button remains visible as a reminder. Click to exit fullscreen and access annotations.

B

Compact overlay in full webpage + fullscreen

In full webpage / fullscreen, sidebar becomes a compact panel (narrower, semi-transparent) pinned to the right edge of the video. Re-parented into the fullscreen element. Full sidebar in normal/widescreen.
