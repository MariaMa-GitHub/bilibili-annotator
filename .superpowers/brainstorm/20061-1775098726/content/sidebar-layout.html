Key behaviors

  • Toggle button: Small tab on the edge, always visible. Click to open/close.
  • Watch progress: Shown at top of sidebar, always visible regardless of active tab.
  • Tabs: 标注 (Annotations), 摘要 (Summary), 标签 (Tags), 评分 (Rating)
  • Timestamp click: Seeks video to that time
  • Range annotations: Show start → end timestamps
  • Category badges: Color-coded — 精彩 (orange), 重要 (green), 搞笑 (pink), 笔记 (blue), 自定义 (gray)
  • 分P support: When video has multiple parts, annotations are filtered by current part number
